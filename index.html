About Paluguntipalli Village

Paluguntipalli is a picturesque village located in Andhra Pradesh, known for its serene environment and vibrant culture. The village is surrounded by lush greenery and offers a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

The village is home to several historic temples and is known for its traditional festivals and occasions, which are celebrated with great enthusiasm and joy by the locals.

Main Events

Peerla Panguda is the main event celebrated in Paluguntipalli village during every Moharam. It is a traditional event marked by vibrant processions, religious rituals, and cultural performances. The event brings together the community in a spirit of unity and devotion.
